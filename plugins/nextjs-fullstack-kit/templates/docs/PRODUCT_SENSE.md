## Product Principles

1. **Speed > Features** — a fast, simple product beats a slow, feature-rich one
2. **Explicit > Clever** — no magic; users should understand what's happening
3. **Mobile-first** — design for the smallest screen, scale up
4. **Errors are UX** — every error state needs a clear message and recovery path

## What We Don't Build

- No gamification or achievement systems
- No social features unless core to the product
- No AI features that can't be explained to the user
- No integrations without a clear user request

## Tone & Copy Guidelines

- **Voice**: Professional but approachable. No jargon.
- **Buttons**: Use verbs ("Save changes", not "Submit")
- **Errors**: Say what went wrong + what to do ("Couldn't save. Check your connection and try again.")
- **Empty states**: Always include a CTA ("No projects yet. Create your first one.")
